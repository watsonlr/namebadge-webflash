How to Flash Your Namebadge

Note: This flasher only works when run on one of the following browsers: Edge, Chrome, Chromium.
  1. Connect your badge to your computer using a USB-C cable (see below).
  2. Hold the BOOT button, then press and release the RESET button.
  3. Release the BOOT button after a second.
  4. Select the desired bootloader version below and click Flash Version.
  5. Follow on-screen instructions. (Use a Chromium-based browser.)
